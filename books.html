Raajnitisangai Raajkaaj

Published in 2021, the book is a collection of column-essays revolving around the idea of Welfare Democracy, which is explained in length and is proposed as an alternative to the ideas of socialism and liberal capitalism. The essays focus on the need of free and quality public education, health, social security and goes on to discuss various other aspects of state craft like infrastructure, urban development, economy, foreign policy, electoral reform, corruption reduction and various social and political issues facing Nepal.
